About Swahili • Kuhusu Kiswahili

Language of Connection / Lugha ya Uhusiano

Kiswahili connects East Africa across business, travel, media, and friendship. It is expressive, musical, and deeply rooted in coast-to-city culture.

  • Spoken by over 200 million people across East and Central Africa.
  • Useful for local life in Nairobi: transport, markets, work, and social circles.
  • Built from rich coastal history with Arabic, Bantu, and global influences.
